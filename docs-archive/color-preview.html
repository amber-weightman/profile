Color Theory Notes:

Each off-white and dark background is carefully chosen to harmonize with its gradient:

  • Warm palettes (1, 3, 5, 6) use cream/ivory tints with warm undertones
  • Cool palettes (2, 4, 7, 8) use whites with blue/gray undertones
  • Dark mode backgrounds are tinted with the gradient's dominant hue for cohesion
  • Text colors in dark mode are lightened versions of the primary gradient color
  • All combinations maintain WCAG AA contrast ratios for accessibility

How to Apply Your Choice:

Once you pick a palette, I'll update your styles.css with the gradient colors and appropriate light/dark mode backgrounds.
